What's so good about HTML5? Here are top 3 benefits of HTML5

  • Improved semantics with new structural elements.
  • Native support for audio, video, and interactive elements.
  • Enhanced forms with built-in validation and new input types.

If you are looking to create forms in your website, these steps to create a great form will be useful

  1. Plan your fields and structure for usability.
  2. Use labels and placeholders for guidance.
  3. Apply native HTML5 attributes for validation.
